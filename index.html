Red velvet crinkles with cream cheese filling

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups (310g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (25g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda, 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups (250g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120g) buttermilk
  • Powdered sugar (for coating)

    • For Cream Cheese:

  • 8 oz (225g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Chocolate Coated Graham Treats

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (200g) crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) evaporated milk or fresh milk (optional, for texture adjustment)
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract (optional, for added flavor)
  • Mini marshmallows (for filling, optional)

    • For Coating:

  • 1 cup (200g) melted chocolate (milk, dark, or white, as preferred)
  • Assorted sprinkles (rainbow, chocolate, or themed sprinkles)
  • Crushed nuts (optional)
  • Shredded coconut (optional)

Stylish Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (25g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) buttermilk (or substitute with 1/2 cup milk + 1/2 tsp vinegar, let sit for 5 mins)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) hot water or hot coffee (for enhanced chocolate flavor)

    • For the Frosting:

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 ½ cups (440g) powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup (50g) cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) heavy cream or milk (adjust for consistency)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Gold Flakes

Number Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups (320g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (100g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk (or 1 cup milk + 1 tbsp vinegar, let sit for 5 minutes)
  • 1 cup (240ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) hot water or coffee (to enhance the chocolate flavor)

    • For the Frosting:

  • 2 cups (450g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups (500g) powdered sugar
  • 1 cup (100g) cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) heavy cream or milk (adjust as needed)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

    • For the Toppings:

  • Assorted chocolate snacks (KitKat, Ferrero Rocher, M&Ms, Oreos, etc.)
  • Chocolate sprinkles
  • Mini marshmallows (optional)

Mocha Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (25g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) buttermilk (or 1/2 cup milk + 1/2 tsp vinegar, let sit for 5 mins)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee or espresso powder dissolved in 1/2 cup (120ml) hot water

    • For the Mocha cream:

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups (360g) powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup (25g) cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp instant coffee or espresso powder dissolved in 2 tbsp hot water
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream or milk (adjust for consistency)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
